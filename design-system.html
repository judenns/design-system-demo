Body Text

Body Large (32px) - This text style is used for prominent paragraphs and featured content.

<p class="body-large">Body Large</p> | font-family: var(--body-font); font-size: var(--fs-large); font-weight: var(--fw-default);

Body Default (20px) - This is the standard paragraph text used throughout the application. It provides good readability on both desktop and mobile devices.

<p>Body Default</p> | font-family: var(--body-font); font-size: var(--fs-default); font-weight: var(--fw-default);

Body Small (16px) - This smaller text is used for secondary information, captions, and supporting content.

<p class="body-small">Body Small</p> | font-family: var(--body-font); font-size: var(--fs-small); font-weight: var(--fw-default);
